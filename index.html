The Holy Hermitage and Monastery at Camaldoli

The ancestral seat of the Camaldolese monastic order

About the Monastery and Order

The Holy Hermitage and Monastery at Camaldoli was founded in the year 1012 by Saint Romuald. It is located near the town of Arezzo in the Italian region of Tuscany.

The Camaldolese order is a Benedictine order. Notable members have included Bede Griffths and David Steindl-Rast.

There is also an old-fashioned pharmacy on the monastery grounds.
